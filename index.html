About Us

The FIRST Alumni Club is a vibrant community of former participants in FIRST robotics programs. We foster lifelong connections, professional growth, and opportunities to give back to the STEM community. Whether you were part of FLL, FTC, or FRC, join us to stay engaged and make a difference!
